MEng Aerospace Engineering student at Queen's University Belfast, currently on placement at Martin-Baker Aircraft — the world leader in ejection seat technology, whose systems have saved over 7,800 lives. My work focuses on safety-critical mechanical design, combining data analysis and stakeholder-facing reporting to drive process improvements across the engineering team.

Outside of placement, I'm a member of Queen's Propulsion Laboratory (QPL), where I run aerodynamic and structural CFD simulations in ANSYS Fluent and SolidWorks to optimise rocket geometry. I also served as Society Secretary from 2024–2025, helping grow QPL to 65+ members across competitive rocketry teams.

I'm building toward a career in UK defence and aerospace, with a particular interest in roles where engineering decisions carry real weight — whether that's a missile system performing under extreme conditions, a rocket clearing the tower, or an ejection seat firing in under 0.3 seconds.
